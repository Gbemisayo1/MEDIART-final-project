Smart Perfume Diffuser

The Smart Perfume Diffuser is a sustainable, app-connected device designed to let users test and explore commercial fragrances at home without relying on wasteful single-use sample vials. By combining microfluidic scent blending with an elegant physical diffuser and an intuitive mobile interface, it offers accurate digital-physical fragrance previews and optional creative scent layering. This system addresses environmental challenges in the perfume industry while aligning with trends in luxury technology, personalization, and eco-conscious consumption.

Role: Led function analysis, IA, and UI design for the Mira app, including wireframing, color palette, typography, and an interactive prototype for class use. Also conducted research on the technologies enabling the diffuser’s functionality.
