Data Visualisation Projects

Redesigning Visualisation

Purpose: Redesigns an AWS climate visualization to improve clarity, accessibility, and interpretability for users.

Process: Uses Python (pandas, numpy, plotly) with simulated data to create three interactive charts, a heatmap, snake race bar chart, and radial bar chart, each highlighting different climate patterns.

Findings: The redesigned visuals reveal clearer seasonal and long-term CDD trends, enhance user engagement, and align with FAIR principles for accessible scientific communication.

Game Influence on Climate Concern

Purpose: Investigates whether playing environmentally themed video games influences climate awareness.

Process: Combines in-game player behavior data with global climate opinion datasets for analysis.

Findings: Interactive visualizations reveal correlations between game behavior and real-world environmental perception.
